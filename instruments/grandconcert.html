Grand Concert

Based off of its slightly smaller counterpart, this guitar also produces a full range of sound with excellent dynamics and response. The difference is that that this model's features better suit players seeking a guitar that excels with a more aggressive finger-style playing or who want something that does well with both finger-style and flat-picking. The slightly longer and wider lower bout allow for a top which can be driven harder, without loosing response.

Specifications

  • Depth at heel: 3 11/32"
  • Depth at end block: 3 27/32"
  • upper bout: 11 3/8"
  • Waist : 9 1/8"
  • lower bout: 15 1/4"
  • Sound hole: 3 7/8"
  • Body Length: 19"
